About us

Ryder's Sports Academy- Where victories begin. A new age Sports Education and Training Academy, Ryders is set up with a passion to get today's kids to embrace sports and more importantly a healthier lifestyle. It is the place where strength finds its way from sore muscles and sweat gives way to success.

At our multi-sport facility, we make it a mission to turn every player on our turf into a stronger athlete, a better teammate and a more confident person. What we believe is that is that champions are born when the spectators aren't watching. Thus we provide our athletes with support, training and a year- round practice facility for putting in the hard work and sweat so that it pays off when game day finally arrives. Rather than relying on a ‘one size fits all’ approach, our dedicated and hard working coaches focus on innovative drills and exercises to suit every trainee’s individual needs.

Ryder’s Sports Academy is a group of dedicated professional sports persons, highly qualified to cater to the needs and requirements of sports programmes and persons.

At Ryder’s Sports Academy ( RSA ) we run various sporting programmes for all walks of life, some of our current undertakings are:

  1. Tennis coaching for children and adults.
  2. Horse Riding lessons
  3. Cricket Coaching
  4. Basketball Coaching
  5. Football Coaching
  6. Roller Skating
  7. Taekwondo Coaching
  8. Table Tennis Coaching
  9. Golf Coaching
  10. Free Tennis Programme for underprivileged children

Presently we are running Eight sports centres in New Delhi & Gurgaon. We have more than 18,000 members registered with us so far and another 4000 odd non-members taking our services on and off
